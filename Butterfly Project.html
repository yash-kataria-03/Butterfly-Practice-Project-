Different Species

Transparent Butterfly

The image of the transparent butterfly (Greta oto) speaks for itself. This species belongs to the family Nymphalidae and is native to Central America.

Zebra Butterfly

The zebra butterfly (Heliconius charithonia) is a sedentary species of butterfly that lives in Florida and South Texas.

The chimera with bird's wings

Chimpanzee Ornithoptera lives in the mountains of New Guinea. Its scientific name, which would come to mean something like "chimera with bird wings" refers to the appearance of the males this spectacular species of butterfly.
